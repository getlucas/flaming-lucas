Chile Time 2013 - 2014

Start times Winter 2013: The Department of Energy reports that have been published in the Official Journal, the Supreme Decree No. 153, the Ministry of the Interior and Public Security, February 6, 2013, which establishes the dates for changes time that will be in effect during the year

Through an official statement reported the following: 
a. - In Chile and in Chilean Antarctic continent.
At 24:00 on Saturday April 27, 2013, is delayed one hour becoming 11:00 the same day.  (GMT - 04).

b. - In Easter Island and Salas y Gomez Island. 
At 22:00 on Saturday April 27, 2013, is delayed one hour becoming 21:00 the same day.  (GMT - 06).

 

Subsequently, the Daylight Saving Time (DST) will start:

a. - In Chile and the Chilean Antarctic continent.
At 24:00 on Saturday September 7, 2013, will be advanced one hour becoming 01:00 of Sunday September 8.   (GMT - 03).

b. - In Easter Island and Salas y Gomez Island.  
At 22:00 on Saturday September 7, 2013, going forward one hour to be 23:00 on the same day.  (GMT - 05).

 

Chile Daylight Saving Time: GMT-3

Chile Standard Time: GMT-4

What time zone is Chile in?

Standard Time is 4 hours behind Greenwich Mean Time (GMT-4).  

Does Chile observe Daylight Saving Time?

Chile operates Daylight Saving Time.  The normal rule was from mid-October (First Sunday on or after 9 October) until mid-March (First Sunday on or after 9 March) when the time is 3 hours behind Greenwich Mean Time (GMT-3).  This is currently, however, in an annual state of flux - see notices.
